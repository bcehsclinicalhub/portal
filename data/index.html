UPCC Data Dictionary

Selection Total

Total number of patients conveyed or directed to a UPCC by BCEHS paramedics during the selected time frame and health authority.

Year to Date

Total number of patients conveyed or directed to a UPCC by BCEHS paramedics in the current calendar year.

Patients Accepted by UPCC

Number of patients conveyed or directed to a UPCC by BCEHS paramedics during the selected time frame, broken down by accepting UPCC.

All BCEHS x UPCC Agreements

List of all UPCC sites in the selected health authority and whether they currently accept patients from BCEHS.

Successful BCEHS Referral to UPCC

Breakdown of cases where a UPCC was able or unable to accept a patient from BCEHS. Rejections are categorized as follows:

  • No, Hours/Availability: Outside of site operating hours.
  • No, Clinical: Patient met baseline criteria, but staff felt they required a higher level of care.
  • No, Capacity: Site did not have space or capacity to accept the patient.
  • No, Unable to Contact UPCC: BCEHS was unable to reach the site to attempt referral.
  • No, Other: Captures various other situations.
